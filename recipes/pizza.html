Evan's Pizza Recipe

This is a Pizza recipe created by the master chef, Evan. It is really good. You should use this recipe. If you don't i will stab you... just kidding, smiley face emoji.

Ingrediants

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mazzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread pizza dough onto pan
  2. Spread pizza sauce onto pizza dough
  3. Spread mazzarella cheese onto pizza sauce
  4. Bake pizza at 350F for 19 minutes, or until golden brown
  5. Eat pizza!
